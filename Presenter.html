Your Course Journey — From Curiosity to Capability

Unit Timeline Topic What You'll Learn (In Simple Terms) Key Skills You'll Gain
1 Months 1-2 Understanding Qualitative Research Learn the basics of “qualitative” research (exploring ideas and experiences) and how it differs from research that uses numbers. Describe key features of qualitative research.
Know when to use this type of research.
Understand how “theory” guides your work.
2 Months 3-4 The Art of the Interview Master one-to-one interviewing, a core research skill. We’ll cover how to plan, arrange, and conduct great interviews, even on sensitive topics. Explain when and how to use interviews.
Design an effective interview guide.
Set up and conduct a professional interview.
3 Months 5-6 Research Ethics Discover how to conduct research responsibly and ethically, ensuring you protect the people you are working with. (Learning outcomes to be tailored and confirmed by the lecturer.)
4 Months 7-8 Analysing Your Data Learn how to find patterns and meaning in the information you've gathered. You'll get hands-on practice analysing real qualitative data. Describe different approaches to analysis.
Understand and perform a “thematic” analysis.
Ensure your analysis is thorough and reliable.
5 Months 9-11 Designing Your Own Study Put everything together to design your own qualitative research study from start to finish, from the first question to the final plan. Write clear and focused research questions.
Plan a complete qualitative research study.
Present a compelling summary of your study design.
